Why Choose Smart Expense Manager?

Unlike traditional expense trackers that require tedious manual entry, Smart Expense Manager uses AI to automatically capture and categorize your transactions from bank SMS. This means zero data entry - just install, grant SMS permissions, and let the app do all the work!

Our intelligent categorization engine learns from your spending patterns and accurately assigns transactions to the right categories. You can review, adjust, and customize categories to match your budgeting style.

With beautiful charts and insights, you'll always know where your money is going. Set budgets, receive alerts, and make smarter financial decisions with AI-powered recommendations.

Privacy First:
We take your privacy seriously. All your financial data stays on your device - we never upload your transactions to the cloud or share them with third parties. You have complete control over your data.

Used for AI-powered insights generation (optional feature). All transaction data remains on your device.
